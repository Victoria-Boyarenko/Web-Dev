Caesar Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Chicken breast (cooked) 1 piece
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 2 tbsp
Caesar dressing 3 tbsp

Steps:

1. Wash and chop the lettuce.

2. Cut the cooked chicken into pieces.

3. Add croutons, cheese, and dressing.

4. Mix everything gently and serve.

Chicken Soup

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3-4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken (pieces) 300 g
Water 1.5 liters
Carrot 1
Potato 2
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Steps:

1. Put the chicken in a pot with water and boil it.

2. Cut the carrot and potatoes.

3. Add vegetables and cook for 20 minutes.

4. Add salt and pepper, then serve hot.
